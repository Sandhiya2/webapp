About MSC Events

MSc Events is a one stop solution for accessing minuscule of information about the events in MSc department.

Our aim is to connect students of the MSc department to participate for various events like Technical, Cultural, Management Fests, Workshops and Seminars.

MSc events provides details about the events, festivals by type or festivals in a month. Students can even register for an event through Msc events itself. We need not to provide separate links for students to register the events. And they can view the list of upcoming offline/online events using this MscFest.
